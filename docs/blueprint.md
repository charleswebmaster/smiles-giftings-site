# **App Name**: Smiles Giftings

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a curated selection of clothing products based on current trends and user preferences.
- Product Filtering & Sorting: Allow users to filter and sort products by type, price, and other relevant attributes.
- Product Details Page: Display detailed product information including high-resolution images, descriptions, and customer reviews.
- Shopping Cart Functionality: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
- Checkout Process: Facilitate a seamless checkout process with options for payment and shipping.
- Newsletter subscription: Allow users to subscribe to a newsletter for latest offers
- AI design suggestion: Use a tool to suggest designs according to current fashion trend.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep maroon (#800000), reflecting richness and elegance, seen in headings and calls to action.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC), offering a neutral backdrop to accentuate products.
- Accent color: Mustard yellow (#FFDB58), strategically used for interactive elements and important highlights.
- Font Pairing: 'Playfair' (serif) for headings to convey elegance, paired with 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text to maintain readability.
- Use of minimalist icons for navigation and product categories, ensuring a clean and intuitive user experience.
- Grid-based layout with clear visual hierarchy, featuring prominent product displays and easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions and hover effects to provide visual feedback and enhance user engagement without being distracting.
